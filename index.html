New York

The Big Apple! Visiting New York city was one of the first things I ticked off my bucket list. I've always loved watching movies and tv series since young and several movies had New York as its setting. The movie lover inside of me had her dream come true. My favourite TV series of all time is Friends, and I was very excited and glad to have seen the Friends Building in NYC.

Fun fact: I almost went to New York University for my undergradate studies.

Deer Park Monastery, San Diego

If you like deers, do not miss a visit to the Deer Park Monastery! The deers here are such dears (pun not intended, I promise.) I visited with my friends from college and we were very lucky that we saw the place at a quiet time. What a peaceful and beautiful place to visit. After being the states for some time, I found out from the locals here that deers are almost as common as birds! Especially along the roads as you drive the long road trips around the country. (This is surprising because you hardly see deers in Singapore.)

Road Trip To Zion National Park

It was such a beautiful day when we drove to the Zion National Park. The weather was so nice and we got to see all the beautiful colours of this nature park.
